TOMB RAIDER 2013 Outfits/Skins and Other Downloadable Content (DLC)

The following downloadable content (DLC) is available for TOMB RAIDER. As far as I know, all are available on PC through Steam, PS3 through the PlayStation Store, and Xbox 360 through the Xbox LIVE Marketplace. If I missed anything, please let me know.

So far the developers have said they have no plans to release any additional single-player DLC. If, like me, you find this unacceptable, I encourage you to make your voice heard by posting a request on the Eidos Forums in this DLC/Feature request thread.

NOTE: The 'details' links go to the Steam site, which contains images and info about the PC DLC. Naturally you can find the same info on the PlayStation Store and Xbox LIVE Marketplace.

SINGLE-PLAYER OUTFITS/SKINS ($0.99/€0.79/80 MSP each)

There are no outfits you can unlock by beating the game. All outfits must be purchased. (Boo!) Also no bikini. (Yay!) Here is a list of the available outfits with images below. Outfits you have bought and downloaded will be available in the Gear menu when you use a base camp, once you've unlocked the option to upgrade Lara's gear in the game.

  • Aviatrix – This outfit dresses Lara up as a classic 1930s adventurer, bomber jacket and all.[details]

  • Demolition – Features an engineering jumpsuit and belt pack. [details]

  • Guerrilla – This outfit makes Lara look like a jungle warfare guerrilla. [details]

  • Hunter – This outfit camouflages Lara in a heavy coating of mud, action movie-style. [details]

  • Mountaineer – The Mountaineer outfit features the padded vest, grapples, and harness of an expert climber. [details]

  • Sure-Shot – With the Sure-Shot outfit, Lara wears a competition style archery suit with high boots and forearm guards. [details]

SINGLE-PLAYER SKILLS/UPGRADES ($0.99/€0.79/80 MSP each)

NOTE:All of the following buyable upgrades can also be unlocked during gameplay. So don't spend money unless you just want to unlock them early.

  • Agility – The Agility skill lets Lara ascend mountain faces more quickly and ensures she lands safely from higher falls (single player upgrade). Same as the unlockable 'Climber's Agility' skill in the game.[details]

  • Animal Instinct – The Animal instinct skill lights up XP-laden animals in Survival Instinct mode, to help Lara upgrade more quickly (single player upgrade). [details]

  • Headshot Reticle – The headshot reticle lets Lara utilize her weapons more accurately. When used with the pistol silencer, it makes for a deadly combination (single player upgrade). [details]

  • Pistol Burst – The three-shot burst pistol upgrade lets Lara unleash a hail of firepower on her foes (single player upgrade). Same as the unlockable 'Burst Fire Mod' in the game. [details]

  • Pistol Silencer – The pistol silencer lets Lara use stealthy tactics to overcome even the trickiest of situations (single player upgrade). Same as the in-game silencer upgrade.[details]
